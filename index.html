learning loop.

You decide to finally learn how to build AI agents. You buy a highly-rated, 40-hour video course.

The first 5 hours are pure theory. Statistics, math, and concepts that feel far removed from building practical tools.

You finally get to code. You copy what the instructor types, but the moment you try to build something unique, you're stuck.
4. The Drop Off
Frustrated and bored, you close the tab. Another course added to the graveyard of unfinished tutorials.

GOT QUESTIONS?

Everything you
need to know.

Is askNora really free? +
Yes. Every lesson, every quiz, every badge, every XP point — completely free. No credit card, no trial, no freemium bait-and-switch. Layers 1–4 are live now with more coming. It stays free.
Do I need a programming background? +
Layer 1 (AI Fundamentals) requires no coding — it's conceptual. Layer 2 onwards assumes you can read Python. You don't need to be a senior engineer, but basic coding comfort helps from Layer 2 onward.
How long does each lesson take? +
Each lesson is designed to be completed in 10 minutes: ~4 minutes reading, ~3 minutes video or diagrams, ~3 minutes quiz. It's built to fit in a lunch break or a commute. No 6-hour marathon sessions.
What if I miss a day and lose my streak? +
You get streak freeze tokens. Use one and your streak stays alive for a missed day. Life happens — we built the streak system to be encouraging, not punishing.
What is NORA? +
NORA is your AI learning companion — a little robot character that reacts to your progress, celebrates milestones, and keeps you motivated. She's not a chatbot. She's part of what makes askNora feel like a game rather than a course.
When will Layers 5–7 be available? +
Layers 5 (AI Infra & Ops), 6 (Multimodal AI), and 7 (AI for Business) are in production and releasing progressively through 2026. Sign up and you'll get notified the moment they drop.
